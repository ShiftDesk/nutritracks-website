Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Can't find what you need? Just email us.

How do I log a meal?

Tap the + button on the home screen and choose how you'd like to add food: scan a barcode, search our database by name, or select a recent or favourite item.

You can also create custom foods and recipes from the My Foods tab if you cook at home regularly.

Can I sync NutriTracks with Apple Health?

Yes! Go to Settings → Integrations → Apple Health and toggle on the data types you'd like to sync. All syncing is optional and you control exactly what is shared.

How do I change my daily calorie or macro goals?

Navigate to Settings → My Goals. You can let NutriTracks calculate targets automatically from your profile, or enter custom targets for calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, and fibre.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password? and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you used Sign in with Apple, use that same method — no password required.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently erases all your personal data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

If you're having trouble, email support@nutritracks.app and we'll process it manually.

Is NutriTracks free to use?

NutriTracks is free to download with a generous set of core features. A NutriTracks Pro subscription unlocks advanced analytics, unlimited custom recipes, and priority support.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → find NutriTracks → tap Cancel Subscription. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

The app is crashing — what should I do?

Force-quit the app and reopen it. If the issue persists, make sure you're on the latest App Store version. Still crashing? Email us with your device model and iOS version.
